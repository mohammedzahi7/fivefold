boAt India

Challenge: boAt India needed to drive regional interest for their audio devices without diluting their historic, high-end branding.

Solution: We filmed a high-contrast macro ad focusing on physical speaker diaphragms and dynamic movements, utilizing custom camera rig structures and golden lighting setups.

Results: The commercial hit 2.4M organic impressions on YouTube and Instagram Reels, driving a 180% increase in local store inquiries.

Challenge: Put the misty heights of Vattavada's hills on the map for luxury eco-travelers during off-season monsoon months.

Solution: Conducted sunrise drone operations under heavy mist, capturing slow-motion cinematic runs and editing them to an atmospheric soundscapes sequence.

Results: Over 800K reach within weeks, resulting in a spike in boutique resort bookings across the Vattavada hill region.
